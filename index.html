Retail Technology Trends

Retail is entering a decisive phase. The question for 2026 is no longer *“Which new technologies should we try?”* It is *“Which technologies belong at the core of how retail operates?”

Retail-as-a-Service (RaaS)

Retail is changing faster than most organizations are built to respond. Consumers expect convenience, personalization, instant fulfillment and seamless cross-channel experiences. Meanwhile, brands are moving beyond transactional selling into service-driven, recurring, subscription-based value models.

Commerce Data Lake

The concept of a Commerce Data Lake has been associated with the largest retailers with expansive digital teams and multi-million-dollar tech budgets. But the reality of modern retail has shifted. Today, mid-market retailers face the same operational complexity, channel fragmentation and customer expectations as their enterprise counterparts.
